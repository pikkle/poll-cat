Interact with your audience !

How it works

Polling

If you need direct feedback for a question, give your audience the Room link ! They will then be able to respond to any question you ask them.

Questions

Some people in your audience might not want to interrupt you, this is a way for them to ask you easily any question or give some remarks.

Interactivity

As soon as a question or a remark is posted, other people can comment it ! Some people in your audience can even answer a question without you !
